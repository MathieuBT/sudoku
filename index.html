Controls

You can play with the mouse or the keyboard.

  • Keys 1 to 9 to fill a cell.
  • Backspace, Delete, or 0 to erase.
  • Notes mode adds several candidates to an empty cell.
  • The Check button flags current conflicts.

About this Sudoku game

Sudoku is a logic-based number puzzle played on a 9x9 grid split into nine 3x3 boxes. The goal is to fill every row, column, and box with the digits 1 through 9 without repeating a number. This free online version generates a fresh puzzle every time, so you can play as many games as you like at your own pace.

Frequently asked questions

Is this Sudoku game free to play?

Yes, this online Sudoku is completely free, with no sign-up or download required.

Can I play Sudoku with the keyboard?

Yes, use the number keys 1-9 to fill a cell, Backspace or Delete to erase, arrow keys to move around the grid, and the N key to toggle notes mode.

What difficulty levels are available?

You can choose between easy, medium, and hard difficulty before starting a new grid.

Does the game save my progress?

Each new grid starts fresh in your browser. Use the Check button anytime to spot conflicts and the Solve button to reveal the full solution.
